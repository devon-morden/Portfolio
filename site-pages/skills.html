Project Management

Formal Training
As part of my certification in instructional design, I learned to work with both waterfall and agile approaches to project management and development. Within the field of instructional design, the waterfall approach is frequently represented through the acronym ADDIE (Analyze, Design, Develop, Implement, and Evaluate). While this is the traditional approach to project managment in instructional design, it can hit major road bumps when design change requests arise halfway through the development stage. Instructional Designers are increasingly moving towards agile approaches like SAM (successive approximation model). All of these formats were covered extensively through my certification, and I have gone on to complete two additional courses in project management.
